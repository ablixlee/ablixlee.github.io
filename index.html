Hamerhead sharks

Hamerhead sharks are one of the most recognizable sharks in the sea.
Hamerhead sharks can see all around them. Their mouth is small compared to their body.
They can weigh from 3 - 550kg.They are an active hunter rather than a scavenger.

  • Scientific name: Sphyrnidae
  • Class: Chondrichthyes
